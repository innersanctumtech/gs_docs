About the App

Geometria Sacra is a meditation app featuring 15 sacred geometry images displayed as a timed slideshow with classical background music. Each image is shown for 60 seconds with gentle Ken Burns transitions, creating a contemplative visual experience.

How to Use

  • Start: Tap "Begin" on the splash screen to start the slideshow
  • Pause/Resume: Tap once anywhere during playback
  • Flip image: Double-tap the center of the screen to reveal the symbol meaning
  • Mute: Tap the speaker icon in the bottom-left corner
  • Info: Tap the info icon in the top-right corner
  • Language: Toggle between English and Portuguese in the top-left corner

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove ads?

Tap the info icon during playback or on the splash screen, then tap "Remove Ads" to make a one-time purchase. This permanently removes all banner advertisements.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the info panel and tap "Restore Purchase." Your ad-free status will be restored automatically if you previously purchased the upgrade using the same Apple ID.

The app is not playing audio.

Make sure your device is not in Silent Mode (check the physical mute switch on the side of your device). Also check that the in-app mute button (speaker icon, bottom-left) is not active.

Can I skip to a specific image?

The app is designed as a continuous meditation experience. Each image plays for 60 seconds in sequence. There are no skip or navigation controls by design.

What data does the app collect?

The app itself does not collect personal data. The free version displays ads via Google AdMob, which may collect device and advertising data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
